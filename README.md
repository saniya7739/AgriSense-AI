🌾 AgriSense AI

🚜 AI-Powered Farming Assistant for Smart Agriculture

AgriSense AI is an intelligent farming assistant designed to empower farmers with modern digital tools for agriculture. The platform provides integrated support for crop planning, disease detection, weather advice, market insights, government schemes, and policy compliance — all accessible through a simple multilingual interface.

The goal of AgriSense AI is to make agricultural decision-making easier, faster, and more accessible for farmers, helping them reduce losses and improve productivity.

✨ Features
🌱 Core Modules
📊 Dashboard

Central hub where farmers can access all platform features and view key farming insights.

🤖 AI Kisan Mitra

AI-powered farming assistant providing personalized agricultural advice through text and voice.

🌾 Crop Management

Tools for crop selection, crop library access, planning schedules, and farming recommendations.

🦠 Disease Detection

AI-assisted crop disease identification with treatment suggestions to help farmers take early action.

🌦 Weather Advice

Weather forecasts combined with farming guidance based on temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind.

📈 Market Prices

Access to crop market prices, including current, highest, lowest, and average rates.

🏛 Government Schemes

Information about important agricultural schemes such as:

PM-Kisan

PMFBY

Soil Health Card

Kisan Credit Card

PMKSY

👨‍🌾 Farmer Community

A collaborative platform where farmers can share questions, experiences, and farming knowledge.

🚨 Emergency Alerts

Critical alerts for pest outbreaks, extreme weather events, and other agricultural emergencies.

🚚 Request Pickup

Allows farmers to request produce pickup and coordinate logistics for selling crops.

🌱 Soil & Fertilizer Guidance

Soil-based fertilizer recommendations and nutrient management suggestions.

🛡 Compliance & Data Policy Module
📑 Policy Management

Upload and manage agricultural policy documents in PDF format.

⚠ Violation Detection

Automatically detect potential policy compliance issues.

👨‍⚖ Human Review Workflow

Allow administrators to review and verify system-detected violations.

📋 Audit Reports

Generate professional compliance reports with export functionality.

🔧 Remediation Panel

Provides recommendations to resolve detected compliance issues.

🌍 Additional Features

🌐 Multilingual Support
Hindi, English, Bhojpuri, Marathi, Punjabi

📱 Mobile Responsive Design
Optimized for smartphones used by farmers.

📡 Offline Capabilities

Clone or download the repository

Open index.html in your web browser

Select your preferred language

Enter your mobile number or continue as guest

Access the dashboard and explore features
